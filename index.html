I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Glasgow, supervised by Michael Wemyss. My research interests centre around homological algebra and representation theory, as well as noncommutative algebraic geometry. I am currently thinking about A-infinity algebras associated to contractions of threefolds. I am also working on a joint project about Paquette-Yıldırım categories and their relation to singularity categories.

I am currently on the job market for academic jobs starting in Fall 2025.
