About me


I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Glasgow, supervised by Michael Wemyss. My research interests centre around homological algebra and representation theory, as well as noncommutative algebraic geometry. I am currently thinking about derived equivalences and autoequivalences between threefolds with canonical singularities. I am also working on a joint project about Paquette-Yıldırım categories and their relation to singularity categories.

Feel free to contact me at m dot purri-brant-godinho dot 1 at research dot gla dot ac dot uk .
