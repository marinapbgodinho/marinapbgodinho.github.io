About me


I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Glasgow, supervised by Michael Wemyss . My interests centre around homological algebra, representation theory as well as noncommutative algebraic geometry. I am currently thinking about derived equivalences and autoequivalences between canonical threefold flops. I am also working on a joint project about Paquette-Yıldırım categories and their relation to singularity categories.
